    Venue Accomodations

     

     

    The organizer will ensure 2 clean and comfortable dressing rooms, which will be at the disposal of the artists upon their arrival. These dressing rooms must have the possibility of being locked by the artists. SUZE SIMS' dressing room should be in a calm environment and must have a lighted mirror, chairs, electrical outlets, running water, and 4 clean towels. Also : 1.5 liter bottles of water, a thermos of hot water, honey, whole lemons, mugs and glasses. Fresh and dried fruits are appreciated. Please do not provide or prepare any food or beverages with nuts.

    Musicians' dressing room (for 5 people) should be equipped with running water, 8 clean towels, electrical outlets, a mirror, and several chairs. Also : mineral water (both uncarbonated and carbonated), hot coffee and tea, beer (Amber or Belgian Microbrews), soft drinks (not Soda Coke Pepsi), fresh and dried fruit and other snacks (potato chips, pretzels, etc).

    No more than 45 minutes after the concert: the Organizer will provide 7 hot meals to be composed of fish, poultry, or beef (no veal  and no pork), fresh vegetables, salad, desert, wine, coffee, and tea. Dinner should be within close proximity of the venue. SUZE SIMS may option the Organizer provide these hot meals during an intermission period no shorter than one 1/2hour.

    To be furnished by the Organizer :

  • The sound system and competent techs for setting up and tearing down.

  • A qualified sound engineer will be present throughout the duration of the concert and will ensure excellent sound quality.

  • Sound and lights will already be installed upon the arrival of the musicians (around 5 p.m. the afternoon of the concert).

  • The sound engineer must be available during set up and sound check.

  • For proper diffusion, the amplification must be adapted to the size of the venue or open air site.
