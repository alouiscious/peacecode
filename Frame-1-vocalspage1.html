Rock Blues Jazz @ 90% original compositions. She is especially talented when it comes to keep a song true to it intended form which is likely why she is asked back by so many of the collaborators, voice over and other talent buyers. When she is not trying to carve out a living; Suze Sims has done many shows with many styles including celebrity impersonation and cookie-cut top 40 Rock and R&B.

The largest focus for this world class singing and scatting talent had been toward the tenure with singer songwriter Teri Wilson of Seattle. Combined these two have produced nearly 40 compositions resembling radio ready adult contemporary roots rocking blues. Together they have self-produced three CD titles and managed over 1000 live productions all of which have heralded much acclaim.
For the last ten years she has recorded for Garey Shelton (GRAMMY award winning producer and engineer), Tom Pfaeffel mastering mixologist who has work with Heart Queensryche and Edison White Snake and many others. Most recently she was commissioned by Bonnie Hayes (GRAMMY award winning composer of Bonnie Raitt's 'Have a Heart' and 'Love Letters').  She recounts going to Bonnie Hayes studios to try to get something hot from her own original collaborations, and being hired on the spot for piece work on  Bonnie's other project(s). Ms Hayes was so impressed she offered her own composition for the next Sims Wilson recording project. Artist Release Scheduled: June 15 2007.
