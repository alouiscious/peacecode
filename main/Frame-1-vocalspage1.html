Over the years Suze has done many shows with many styles including celebrity impersonation and cookie-cut top 40 Rock and R&B. When she is not trying to carve out a living, you can catch her vocalizing Jazz Blues and original tunes. She is especially talented when it comes to keep a song true to it intended form which is likely why she is asked back by so many of the collaborators, voice over and other talent buyers.

 

For the last ten years she has recorded for Garey Shelton (GRAMMY award winning producer and engineer), Tom Pfaeffel mastering mixologist who has work with Heart Queensryche and Edison Motley Crue and many others. Most recently she was hired by Bonnie Hayes (GRAMMY award winning composer of Bonnie Raitt's 'Have a heart' and 'Love Letters').  She recounts going to Bonnie Hayes studios to try to get something hot from her own original collaborations, and being hired on the spot for piece work on  Bonnie's other project(s).

The largest focus for this World class singing and scatting talent had been toward the tenure with singer songwriter Teri Wilson of Seattle. Combined these two have produced nearly 40 compositions resembling radio ready adult contemporary roots rocking blues. Together they have self-produced three CD titles and managed over 1000 live productions all of which have heralded much acclaim.
