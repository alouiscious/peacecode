THE RED HOTZ CD ART CONTEST
OFFICIAL RULES

1) Entry Period: The Red Hotz CD Art Contest (the "Contest") begins at 12:01 a.m. Eastern Time ("ET") on April 17, 2007 and ends at 11:59 a.m. ET on May 3, 2007 ("Entry Period").

2) Eligibility: Contest is open to legal residents of the 50 United States and District of Columbia who are 13 years of age or older at time of Entry.  An individual cannot be under contract to any label, company or other person or entity which may have proprietary interest in individual's work. Employees, managers and agents of The Red Hot Blues Sisters ("The Red Hotz"), The Peace Code Records Group, and their respective parents, subsidiaries, affiliates and advertising and promotional agencies, and the immediate families (spouse, and parents, siblings and children, and each of their respective spouses, regardless of where they live) and persons living in the households of each of these individuals, whether related or not, are ineligible to enter or win the prize. An eligible minor should have a parent's or legal guardian's permission to enter.  Void outside the 50 United States and District of Columbia and where prohibited by law. The Contest is subject to all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations.

3) How to Enter: Visit www.theredhotz.com and click on "The Red Hotz CD Art Contest" link for complete details.  You will be asked to submit digital artwork for up to four(4) panels and one(1) compact disk suggestions for the packaging of the next new full-length 2007 CD release  During the Entry Period, complete & submit the official Entry form available at www.theredhotz.com and follow the directions to submit your artwork entry.  CD Artwork entries can only be submitted in CMYK Adobe Photoshop JPEG format (with layers unlocked and un-flattened) with an official entry form on-line. Please note that all official entry form on-line are available for submission now www.theredhotz.com.  One (1) Entry allowed per person, however, each additional Entry must be original and unique from any other Entry submitted by such entrant, as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion.  All Entries become the property of the Sponsor and will not be acknowledged or returned.

CD Artwork Guidelines:

  • Samples OR INTERPOLATIONS are prohibited. Any CD Artwork containing      samples or interpolations will be disqualified.
  • CD Artwork must be submitted in CMYK Adobe Photoshop JPEG format (with layers unlocked and un-flattened)

Contest Entry Guidelines:

  • Entry must be entrant's original creation, created solely by entrant, with no restrictions or limitations on entrant's right to submit, or Sponsor's right to use, the Entry.  Entry may not have previously won an award or have been previously published.  Entry cannot defame or invade publicity rights or privacy of any person, living or deceased, or otherwise infringe upon any person's personal or proprietary rights. Modifying, enhancing or altering a third party's pre-existing work does not qualify as entrant's original creation.  Entry may not be offensive or inappropriate, as determined by Sponsor, in its sole discretion. Sponsor reserves the right to disqualify any Entry it deems to be inappropriate, offensive or is not in keeping with Sponsor's image, in its sole discretion.

    By submitting an Entry, each entrant (or, if a minor in his/her state of residence, his/her parent or legal guardian) agrees that Sponsor may post his/her Entry on www.theredhotz.com for review by visitors to the website and or subsequent voting processes should the sponsor determine voting for a title choice is neccesary as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion.

    Entries not complying with these Official Rules will be subject to disqualification. No correspondence regarding Entries will be entered into with entrants other than described herein.

    4) Winner Selection:  Initial Judging: All eligible Entries received will be judged by a panel of qualified judges based equally on the following judging criteria: Innovation, Originality, Creativity and Professional Quality, to determine the top ten (10) Entries (the "Finalists"). In the event of a tie, all tied Entries will be re-judged by an additional "tie-breaking" judge based on the criteria listed above and that judge will break the tie. Sponsor's and judges' decisions are final and binding on all matters. Sponsor reserves the right to choose fewer than ten (10) Finalists in the event an insufficient number of eligible and qualified Entries meet the minimum judging criteria are received, as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion.  Final Judging: One or more Finalist Entries will be posted at www.theredhotz.com at 12:01 a.m. ET on May 16, 2007

    5) Winner Notification: Potential winner will be notified by email, phone and/or mail and will be required to execute an Affidavit of Eligibility and Liability Release and, where legal, a Publicity Release, and return same, fully-executed, within seven (14) days of date of issuance. In the event of noncompliance within this time period, these Official Rules or if the prize notification is unclaimed or returned as undeliverable, the prize may be forfeited and, at Sponsor's discretion.
